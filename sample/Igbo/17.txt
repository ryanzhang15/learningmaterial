Oro Agri enwetala ọtụtụ ihe ndebanye aha maka azụmahịa anyị na Europe na pọtụfoliyo anyị niile.


Otu onye otu OMNIA bụ Oro Agri International Ltd, nwere obi ụtọ ịkpọsa na anyị enwetala ọtụtụ ndebanye aha maka azụmahịa anyị na Yurop na mpaghara anyị niile.

Na ụdị biostimulants, anyị nwetara aha ndebanye aha OROMATE K26 na France na Belgium, dabere na nnabata nkwenye dịka ikike nke OROMATE K26 na Portugal.

WETCIT NEO, ọtụtụ adjuvant anyị, tinyekwa ndebanye aha ọhụrụ na ndepụta nke mba ebe enyere ya ikike, ma ọ dịla njikere kesaa ya na Ireland.

Banyere ngwaahịa nchekwa osisi anyị, anyị nwetara ndebanye aha maka PREV-GOLD na Czech Republic na Germany, dị ka Insecticide na Miticide maka iji griin ha, yana na France dị ka Insecticide, Miticide na Fungicide maka ọhịa. N'ime otu a, anyị nwetakwara ndebanye aha maka OROCIDE PLUS na Sweden dị ka Ahụhụ ahụhụ na Miticide maka iji griin ha.

Matakwu banyere ngwaahịa ndị a na ngwaahịa ndị ọzọ sitere na ORO AGRI biko gaa na nke anyị peeji nke ngwaahịa ma ọ bụ kpọtụrụ onye nnọchi anya ORO AGRI dị na mpaghara gị na onye nkesa.
